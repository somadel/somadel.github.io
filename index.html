Overview – SOMADEL

With a strong technical foundation, SOMADEL delivers reliable, safe, and efficient solutions tailored to modern electrical infrastructure needs.

Core Activity

Electrical engineering solutions, cable management systems, and industrial infrastructure under our brand SOMAFIL.

Technical Excellence

Compliance with standards: ISO 1416, NFA 97-121, NFA 91-122. Manufactured with top-quality materials.

Value Proposition

High-quality manufacturing, flexible customization, and efficient cable management solutions.

Features & Advantages

Our solutions are used in industrial installations, commercial buildings, electrical networks, and infrastructure projects.

Safety & Protection

Non-conductive insulation, safe for personnel, and complete electrical isolation from masses.

Durability & Resistance

Moisture and corrosion-resistant galvanized steel with strong mechanical resistance.

Performance & Design

Excellent cable ventilation, lightweight mesh structure (50×100 mm), and aesthetically modern.

SOMAFIL Cable Trays

Hot-dip galvanized steel wire covered with non-conductive insulating material. Designed for routing, protection, and organization.

Technical Specs

4.40 mm steel wire. Wide range of dimensions from 50×50 mm to 400×100 mm. Custom dimensions available on request.

Load Capacity

Weight from 0.91 to 2.30 kg/m. Supports up to ~55.6 daN/m on a 1.5m span and ~34.5 daN/m on a 2m span.

Packaging & Handling

Supplied in 2-meter bars, packed in compact plastic bundles for easy transport, fast installation, and reduced storage.
